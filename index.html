The Bride and Groom

BRE

Bre was raised in Bullard, Texas and is the oldest of four children. Bre is a couple months away from a degree in Elementary Education at UT Tyler. Bre is currently student teaching in Bullard and also teaches Sunday School. Bre enjoys reading, writing, and watching butterflies.

KORY

Kory was raised in Wichita Falls, Texas and is the middle child of three brothers. Kory moved to Bullard in 2006, where his family started Greater Life Church. In 2013, Kory graduated from UT Tyler with a degree in Mathematics and is currently the Systems Engineer for Bullard ISD. Kory is also the youth pastor of Elevate Student Ministries. Kory enjoys working on computers and playing the piano.

Kory and Bre both share a love for What About Kabob’s Gyros and chocolate chip cookies.

HOW WE MET

Kory and Bre were in the same grade at Bullard High School. Although they met during their freshman year, the two did not become best friends until their junior year, when they were in a US History AP class with only four students. They became best friends and later fell in love. After graduating high school, Bre moved to College Station to attend Texas A&M. The next year she moved back to Bullard and they started dating. After two and a half years, Kory popped the question, and the rest is history...

THE PROPOSAL

On November 30, 2013, Kory took Bre to Holiday in the Park at Six Flags Over Texas. Bre just wanted a funnel cake, but Kory was determined to get Bre on the Texas Giant. Bre does not like big roller coasters, and she especially does not like the Texas Giant. The last time they were on it, Kory attempted to ask Bre to be his girlfriend but couldn’t because she was terrified. After the ride malfunctioned and they waited an hour, they finally reached the front of the line. Right before dropping, Kory asked Bre to marry him! She didn’t believe him at first but once the ride stopped (and she stopped screaming and crying), she said yes! Kory got down on one knee and asked Bre to be his forever! She said yes a lot quicker this time and in the end, she got her funnel cake!
